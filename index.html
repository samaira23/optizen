Optizen is a student club under the department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, at SRMIST Kattankulathur. We are an official student chapter of Optica, an American society that specializes in photonics and optics.

The club operates under the guidance of Dr. Shanti Prince, former Head of Department, Electronics and Communication Engineering. Optizen has various active projects and ongoing research efforts in a multitude of domains, including Electronics, Embedded Systems, AI/ML, and Optics.
